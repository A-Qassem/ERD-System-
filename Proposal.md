## 1. Project Overview

This proposal outlines the development of a **comprehensive management system for educational centers** with multiple branches.  
The system will cover **attendance tracking**, **payment management**, **scheduling**, and **branch-based reporting**, ensuring smooth operations and financial transparency.

---

## 2. System Features

### **A. Student Attendance Module**
- Each student has a **unique code** used to record attendance.
- Attendance data includes:
  - Student name  
  - Attendance time and date  
  - Subject  
  - Classroom  
  - Branch (center name or ID)  
- Attendance is linked to the correct lesson/session to avoid duplication or confusion.

---

### **B. Staff Module**
- Staff can:
  - Record student attendance.
  - Select the correct session before registering attendance.
  - Modify or reschedule classes for a **specific day, week, month, or term**.
  - Record student payments manually (monthly or per session) using student and subject codes.

---

### **C. Financial Management**
- Track student payments (monthly or per-session basis).  
- Generate detailed reports for **teacher payments and salaries**.  
- Include confirmation and approval for teacher salary disbursement.  

---

### **D. Admin (Head Office) Module**
- **main administrative account** with full control over:
  - Adding and managing **teachers and staff accounts**.  
  - Viewing **financial reports**, expenses, and profits per branch.  
  - Generating **overall performance reports** across all centers.  
  - Monitoring student registration growth or decline.  
  - Viewing detailed branch-level data for attendance, revenue, and student activity.
- **center-based admin** with control over:
  - all specific center's operations (main administrative account) but limited to specific center
---

## 3. Security & Data Protection

To ensure the safety and privacy of all data, the system will include:
- **Role-based Access Control (RBAC):** Each user role (Admin, Staff, Teacher, Accountant) has specific permissions.  
- **Data Encryption:** Sensitive information (student and financial data) will be securely stored and transmitted.  
- **Secure Authentication:** Strong password policies and login validation for all accounts.  
- **Database Backup System:** Regular automated backups to prevent data loss.  
- **Activity Logging:** Record of all important actions for accountability and tracking.

---

## 4. Suggested Additional Features
To enhance scalability and usability:
- **Notification system** for unpaid fees or upcoming sessions.  
- **Dashboard with charts** for real-time performance visualization.  
- **Export reports to PDF/Excel.**  
- **Search and filter options** across all modules.
